# Review & Feedback Agent

## What It Does
After every purchase or visit, automatically asks customers for a review on Google, Zomato, JustDial, or your platform of choice — and collects internal feedback to help you improve.

## Who It Is For
- Restaurants (Google + Zomato reviews)
- Clinics and hospitals (Google reviews)
- Salons (Google + JustDial)
- Retail shops
- Service businesses
- Any business where reviews drive new customers

## Sample Conversations

**2 days after visit:**
> *"Hi [Name]! Thank you for visiting us recently. We hope you had a great experience. Would you mind taking 30 seconds to leave us a review on Google? It really helps us a lot! 🙏 [Google Review Link]"*

**If customer says experience was bad:**
**Agent:** I am really sorry to hear that. Your feedback is very important to us. Can you tell me what went wrong? I will make sure it is addressed personally.
[Escalates to owner — does NOT ask for public review]

## Smart Review Logic
- Happy customer → Ask for public Google/Zomato review
- Unhappy customer → Collect feedback privately → Escalate to owner
- No response → One follow-up after 2 days → Stop

## What the Agent Handles
- Post-visit review request (timed perfectly)
- Platform-specific review links (Google, Zomato, JustDial)
- Negative feedback collection (private — no damage control needed)
- Owner alert for negative feedback
- Review tracking and reporting
