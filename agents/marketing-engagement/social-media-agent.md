# Social Media Manager Agent

## What It Does
Generates and schedules social media posts — captions, hashtags, and optimal posting times — for Instagram, Facebook, and LinkedIn. Keeps your business active on social media without any effort from you.

## Who It Is For
- Small businesses and local shops
- Restaurants and cafes
- Salons and spas
- Coaches and consultants
- Creators and influencers
- Any business that wants consistent social presence

- Optimal posting time scheduling
