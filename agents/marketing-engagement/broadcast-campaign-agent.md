# Broadcast Campaign Agent

## What It Does
Sends personalised promotional messages, offers, and updates to your entire customer list via WhatsApp — with the customer's name and relevant details. Tracks delivery, reads, and responses.

## Who It Is For
- Retail shops
- Restaurants and cafes
- E-commerce businesses
- Salons and spas
- Any business with a customer list

## Sample Broadcasts

**Product Launch:**
> *"Hi [Name]! 🎉 We just launched our new Summer Collection. Exclusive early access for our loyal customers — 20% off for the next 48 hours. Shop here: [link]"*

**Offer Reminder:**
> *"Hi [Name], don't miss out! Our End of Season Sale ends tomorrow. Up to 50% off on selected items. Visit us or order online: [link]"*

**Re-engagement:**
> *"Hi [Name], we miss you! It's been a while. Here's a special ₹200 voucher just for you — valid for the next 7 days. Come back and say hi! 😊"*

## What the Agent Handles
- Scheduled broadcast sending
- Personalised messages with customer name
- Offer code generation and tracking
- Delivery and read receipt tracking
- Response handling for replies
- Opt-out management (TRAI compliance)
- Campaign performance reporting

## Campaign Types
- New product or service launch
- Festival and seasonal offers
- Flash sales
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Loyalty rewards
- Event invitations
- Important updates and announcements

## Compliance
All broadcasts sent via official WhatsApp Business API (AiSensy) — fully compliant with Meta and TRAI guidelines. Opt-out handled automatically.
