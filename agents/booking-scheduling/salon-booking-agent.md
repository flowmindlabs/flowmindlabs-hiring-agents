# Salon & Spa Booking Agent

## What It Does
Books salon and spa services via WhatsApp or Instagram DM — shows available slots, confirms bookings, sends reminders, and handles cancellations automatically.

## Who It Is For
- Salons and hair studios
- Spas and wellness centres
- Beauty parlours
- Nail studios
- Ayurvedic and massage centres

## Sample Conversations

**Customer:** Hi, I want to book a haircut for tomorrow.
**Agent:** Of course! Tomorrow we have slots at 11am, 2pm, and 4:30pm. Which time works for you?

**Customer:** 2pm please.
**Agent:** Confirmed! Your haircut appointment is tomorrow at 2pm. You will get a reminder an hour before. See you then! 💇

**Customer:** Facial aur cleanup ke liye slot chahiye Saturday ko.
**Agent:** Saturday ko facial aur cleanup ke liye humare paas 11am aur 3pm available hai. Kaunsa time suit karega?

## What the Agent Handles
- Service menu and pricing queries
- Slot availability check
- Booking confirmation
- Pre-appointment reminder (1 hour before)
- Cancellation and rescheduling
- Stylist preference (if applicable)
- Special request collection (bridal, event)
- Waitlist for fully booked slots
