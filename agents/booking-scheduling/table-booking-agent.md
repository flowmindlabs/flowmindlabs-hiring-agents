# Table Booking Agent

## What It Does
Takes table reservations via WhatsApp — date, time, party size, and special requests. Confirms instantly, sends reminders, and handles cancellations automatically.

## Who It Is For
- Restaurants
- Cafes and bistros
- Banquet halls
- Hotels and resorts
- Cloud kitchens with dine-in

## Sample Conversations

**Customer:** Hi, I want to book a table for 4 this Saturday at 7pm.
**Agent:** Great! Saturday 12 April at 7pm for 4 people — shall I confirm this booking?

**Customer:** Yes please.
**Agent:** Confirmed! Your table is booked for Saturday 12 April at 7pm for 4 guests. We will send you a reminder on Saturday morning. See you then! 🍽️

**Customer:** Can I cancel my booking?
**Agent:** Of course. Your booking for Saturday 12 April at 7pm has been cancelled. We hope to see you another time!

## What the Agent Handles
- Table availability check
- Booking confirmation
- Cancellation and rescheduling
- Special request collection (birthday, anniversary, dietary needs)
- Automatic reminder 2 hours before booking
- Owner notification for every new booking
