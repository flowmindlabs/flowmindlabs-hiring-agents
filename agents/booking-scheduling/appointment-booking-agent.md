## Sample Conversations

**Patient:** I need to book an appointment with Dr. Mehta.
**Agent:** Dr. Mehta is available on Wednesday 16 April and Friday 18 April. Which day works for you?

**Patient:** Wednesday please.
**Agent:** Dr. Mehta has slots at 10am, 11:30am, and 3pm on Wednesday. Which time suits you?

**Patient:** 11:30am.
**Agent:** Confirmed! Your appointment with Dr. Mehta is on Wednesday 16 April at 11:30am. You will receive a reminder the evening before. Please bring your previous reports if any.
