# Multilingual Customer Support Agent

## What It Does
Handles customer queries in all major Indian languages — Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Marathi, and English. Detects the customer's language automatically and responds in the same language. Routes to the correct regional team when escalation is needed.

## Who It Is For
- Pan-India businesses
- Banks and NBFCs
- Insurance companies
- Telecom providers
- E-commerce with national reach
- Government portals
- Any business serving customers across Indian states
