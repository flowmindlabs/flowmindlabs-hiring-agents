# Sales Follow-Up Agent

## What It Does
Automatically follows up with prospects after demos, sends relevant content, answers product questions, books next meetings, and keeps deals moving — without your sales team lifting a finger.

## Who It Is For
- B2B sales teams
- SaaS companies
- Consulting firms
- Enterprise software vendors
- Any business with a sales pipeline and demo process

## Sample Follow-Up Sequence

**Day 1 (2 hours after demo):**
> *"Hi [Name], thank you for joining today's demo! It was great to understand [Company]'s needs. I am sharing our case study on how we helped [Similar Company] achieve [Result]. Happy to answer any questions — [Sales Rep Name]"*

**Day 3:**
> *"Hi [Name], just following up on the demo. Did you get a chance to review the case study? I would love to address any specific concerns your team might have. Available for a 15-minute call this week?"*

**Day 7:**
> *"Hi [Name], I wanted to share our ROI calculator — plug in your numbers and see the potential impact for [Company]. [Link]. Our offer is valid until end of month. Shall we finalise the next steps?"*

## What the Agent Handles
- Post-demo follow-up sequence
- Relevant content and case study sharing
- Product question answering
- Objection handling (pricing, timeline, competitor comparison)
