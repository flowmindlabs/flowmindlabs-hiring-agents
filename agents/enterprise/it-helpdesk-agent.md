# IT Helpdesk Agent

## What It Does
Handles employee IT requests — password reset, software access, VPN issues, hardware requests, common troubleshooting — and escalates to your IT team only when human intervention is genuinely needed.

## Who It Is For
- Companies with 50+ employees
- Startups with a tech team
- Any organisation with an internal IT support function

## Sample Conversations

**Employee:** I forgot my Outlook password.
**Agent:** No worries! For Outlook password reset, go to: [reset link]. Click "Forgot Password" and use your company email. Takes about 2 minutes. Let me know if you need more help.

**Employee:** I need access to the AWS staging environment.
**Agent:** Got it! Access requests need manager approval. I have raised a ticket (IT-4521) and sent an approval request to your manager. You will get access within 4 business hours once approved.

**Employee:** My laptop is very slow since the last update.
**Agent:** Common issue after updates. Try these steps first:
1. Restart your laptop
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check for background processes (Ctrl+Shift+Esc)
Still slow after that? I will escalate to IT for a remote session.
