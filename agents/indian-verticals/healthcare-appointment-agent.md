# Healthcare Appointment Agent

## What It Does
Books OPD appointments, shares doctor availability, sends reminders, handles prescription query escalation, and reduces no-shows — all via WhatsApp. Built for Indian hospitals and clinics.

## Who It Is For
- Multi-specialty hospitals
- Clinics and polyclinics
- Diagnostic centres
- Ayurvedic and homeopathy clinics
- Dental clinics
- Physiotherapy centres

## Sample Conversations

**Patient:** I need to see a cardiologist.
**Agent:** Of course. Dr. Sharma (Cardiologist) is available on Tuesday and Thursday. Which day works for you?

**Patient:** Tuesday please. Morning if possible.
**Agent:** Dr. Sharma has slots at 9am, 10:30am, and 11am on Tuesday 15 April. Which time suits you?

**Patient:** 10:30am.
**Agent:** Confirmed! Your appointment with Dr. Sharma (Cardiology) is on Tuesday 15 April at 10:30am. Please bring previous reports and arrive 10 minutes early for registration. You will get a reminder the evening before.

**Patient:** Mujhe sugar ki problem hai, kaunsa doctor milega?
**Agent:** Aapko endocrinologist ya general physician se milna chahiye. Hamare yahan Dr. Gupta (Endocrinology) available hain. Kya main appointment book karoon?

## What the Agent Handles
- Speciality-based doctor routing
- Appointment booking with confirmation
- New and returning patient identification
- Reminder (day before + 2 hours before)
- Cancellation and rescheduling
- Basic symptom-based department routing
- Report and test result query escalation
- Emergency escalation to hospital helpdesk

## Important Notes
- Agent does NOT give medical advice
- Symptom queries → Routes to appropriate department only
- Emergency symptoms → Immediately directs to emergency number
