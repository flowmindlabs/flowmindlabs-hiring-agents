# School Parent Communication Agent

## What It Does
Handles all parent queries automatically — fee reminders, exam schedules, holiday notices, result updates, and general school information. Available 24/7 in English and Hindi.

## Who It Is For
- Schools (primary, secondary, senior secondary)
- Coaching institutes
- Play schools and nurseries
- Tuition centres
- Music, dance, and arts academies

## Sample Conversations

**Parent:** When is the next PTM?
**Agent:** The next Parent-Teacher Meeting is on Saturday 19 April from 9am to 1pm. Your slot will be confirmed by the class teacher. Is there anything else you would like to know?

**Parent:** Fees ki last date kya hai?
**Agent:** April term ki fees ki last date 15 April hai. Aap online portal ya school office mein jama kar sakte hain. Koi aur jankari chahiye?

**Parent:** Is school open tomorrow?
**Agent:** Yes, school is open tomorrow. Regular timing 8am to 2:30pm. No holiday declared.
