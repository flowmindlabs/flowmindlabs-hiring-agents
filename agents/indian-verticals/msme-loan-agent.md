# MSME Loan Enquiry Agent

## What It Does
Explains MSME loan eligibility, collects business details, pre-qualifies applicants, answers common loan queries, and routes qualified leads to your loan officer — 24/7 in Hindi and English.

## Who It Is For
- NBFCs (Non-Banking Financial Companies)
- Cooperative banks
- Small finance banks
- MSME-focused lenders
- Government scheme facilitators (Mudra, CGTMSE)

## Sample Conversations

**Business owner:** Mujhe apni dukaan ke liye loan chahiye. Kitna mil sakta hai?
**Agent:** Bilkul! MSME loan ke liye aap ₹50,000 se ₹50 lakh tak apply kar sakte hain. Aapka business kitne saal purana hai?

**Owner:** 3 saal.
**Agent:** Achha! 3 saal purane business ke liye aap eligible hain. Aapka last year ka annual turnover kitna tha approximately?

**Owner:** Around 25 lakh.
**Agent:** Aap ₹5-8 lakh tak ke loan ke liye eligible ho sakte hain. Kya aapke paas GST registration hai?

**Owner:** Haan hai.
**Agent:** Bahut achha! Aap strong applicant hain. Kya main aapko hamare loan officer se connect karoon? Wo aapko exact amount aur interest rate bata sakenge.

## What the Agent Handles
- Loan product explanation (term loan, working capital, equipment finance)
- Eligibility pre-qualification
- Document checklist sharing
- Government scheme information (PM Mudra Yojana, CGTMSE)
- Interest rate range explanation
- Application status queries (for existing applicants)
- Loan officer callback booking

## Compliance Note
Agent provides general information only. All loan decisions are made by your licensed lending officers.
