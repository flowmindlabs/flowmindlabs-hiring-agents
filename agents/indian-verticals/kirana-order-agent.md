# Kirana & Grocery Order Agent

## What It Does
Customers send their grocery list on WhatsApp — in Hindi or English. The agent takes the order, confirms the total, notifies the shop owner, and arranges delivery or pickup.

## Who It Is For
- Kirana stores
- Local grocery shops
- Milk and dairy delivery
- Vegetable and fruit vendors
- Organic food stores

## Sample Conversations (Hindi)

**Customer:** Bhaiya, 2kg aata, 1kg chawal, 1L doodh chahiye.
**Agent:** Ji! Aapka order note ho gaya:
- Aata 2kg — ₹80
- Chawal 1kg — ₹60
- Doodh 1L — ₹25
**Total: ₹165**
Delivery ya pickup?

**Customer:** Delivery chahiye, 6 baje tak.
**Agent:** Perfect! Aapka order 6 baje tak deliver ho jayega. Dhanyawad! 🙏

- Order history for repeat customers
