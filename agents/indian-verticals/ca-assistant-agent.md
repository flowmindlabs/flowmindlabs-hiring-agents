## Sample Conversations

**Client:** Has my ITR been filed for FY 2024-25?
**Agent:** Let me check. Your ITR for FY 2024-25 was filed on 28 July 2025. Acknowledgement number: ITR2025XXXX. Do you need a copy sent to your email?

**Client:** What documents do I need for ITR filing?
**Agent:** For salaried individuals you will need:
- Form 16 from your employer
- Bank statements (all accounts)
- Investment proofs (80C, 80D)
- Home loan statement (if applicable)
- Aadhar and PAN card
Shall I send you the complete checklist on WhatsApp?

**Client:** ITR filing ki last date kya hai?
**Agent:** FY 2024-25 ke liye ITR filing ki last date 31 July 2025 hai (bina penalty ke). Kya aapne abhi tak apne documents submit kiye hain?
