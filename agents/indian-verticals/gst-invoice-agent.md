# GST & Invoice Agent

## What It Does
Answers GST queries, explains tax slabs in simple language, helps with invoice summary understanding, and sends GST filing deadline reminders — for MSMEs, freelancers, and small business owners.

## Who It Is For
- MSMEs and small businesses
- Freelancers and consultants
- Traders and retailers
- CA firms (for client query handling)

## Sample Conversations

**Business owner:** Meri services pe kitna GST lagega? Main ek freelance designer hoon.
**Agent:** Freelance design services pe 18% GST lagta hai (SAC code 998314). Agar aapka annual turnover ₹20 lakh se kam hai, toh aapko GST registration ki zarurat nahi. Kya aap registration ke baare mein aur janna chahte hain?

**Business owner:** GST filing ki last date kab hai?
**Agent:** Monthly GSTR-1 ki due date har mahine ki 11 tarikh hai. GSTR-3B ki due date 20 tarikh. Is month yani April mein:
- GSTR-1: 11 April
- GSTR-3B: 20 April
Kya aapko reminder set karna hai?

**Business owner:** Yes please.
**Agent:** Done! Aapko 3 din pehle reminder milega. Koi aur sawaal?

## What the Agent Handles
- GST slab queries by product or service type
- Registration threshold questions
- Filing deadline reminders

- E-way bill basic queries
