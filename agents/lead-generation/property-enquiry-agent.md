# Property Enquiry Agent

## What It Does
Handles all property enquiries via WhatsApp — location preferences, budget range, BHK requirements, possession timeline, and site visit booking. Qualifies leads automatically and routes hot leads to your sales team instantly.

## Who It Is For
- Real estate developers
- Property brokers and agents
- Housing finance companies
- Co-working space providers
- Commercial property dealers

## Sample Conversations

**Lead:** Hi, I am looking for a 3BHK flat in Whitefield.
**Agent:** Great choice! We have 3BHK options in Whitefield starting from ₹85 lakhs. What is your budget range?

**Lead:** Around 1 to 1.2 crore.
**Agent:** Perfect — we have 3 projects that match your budget. Are you looking for ready possession or under construction?

**Lead:** Ready possession preferred.
**Agent:** Excellent! We have 2 ready possession 3BHKs in Whitefield. Would you like to schedule a site visit this weekend?

**Lead:** Yes, Saturday works.
**Agent:** Great! I will have our sales manager call you to confirm the Saturday visit. Can I get your name and best time to call?

## What the Agent Handles
- Location and BHK preference collection
- Budget qualification
- Ready vs under construction preference
- Site visit scheduling
- Hot lead instant notification to sales team

## Lead Scoring
Agent automatically scores leads:
- **Hot:** Budget confirmed + site visit booked → Instant WhatsApp to sales team
- **Warm:** Interested but timeline unclear → Follow-up sequence triggered
- **Cold:** Just browsing → Nurture sequence over 7 days
