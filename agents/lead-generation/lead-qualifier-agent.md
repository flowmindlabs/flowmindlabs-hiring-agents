## Lead Scoring System

| Score | Criteria | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Hot | Budget confirmed + timeline clear + decision maker | Instant notification to sales |
| Warm | Interested but timeline vague | Follow-up sequence Day 1, 3, 7 |
| Cold | Just exploring | Nurture with content over 2 weeks |
