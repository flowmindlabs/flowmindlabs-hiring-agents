# Follow-Up Agent

## What It Does
Automatically follows up with leads who did not respond — sends personalised reminders at Day 1, Day 3, and Day 7 without any human intervention. Never lets a lead go cold.

## Who It Is For
- Sales teams
- Real estate agents
- Insurance brokers
- EdTech and coaching institutes
- Any business with a sales pipeline

## Sample Follow-Up Sequence

**Day 1 (4 hours after no response):**
> *"Hi [Name], just checking in! We spoke about [product/service] earlier. Happy to answer any questions you might have. Shall we connect?"*

**Day 3:**
> *"Hi [Name], I wanted to share something that might help you decide — [testimonial or key benefit]. Would love to help you move forward. Available for a quick call?"*

**Day 7:**
> *"Hi [Name], this is my final follow-up. Our offer for [product/service] is valid until [date]. Let me know if you would like to proceed — happy to help anytime."*

## What the Agent Handles
- Automatic follow-up at Day 1, 3, 7
- Personalised messages with lead name and context
- Response detection — stops sequence when lead replies
- Hot lead escalation when lead responds positively
- Opt-out handling (compliance)
- Follow-up performance reporting

## Follow-Up Rules
- If lead replies positively → Route to sales team immediately
- If lead says not interested → Stop sequence, mark as closed
- If lead asks question → Answer or escalate
- If no response after Day 7 → Mark as cold, move to nurture list
