# Insurance Lead Agent

## What It Does
Explains insurance policy options in simple language, captures lead details, pre-qualifies applicants, and books callbacks with your agent — handles LIC, term, health, motor, and home insurance queries.

## Who It Is For

- Insurance brokers

## Sample Conversations

**Lead:** Mujhe ek achha term insurance plan chahiye.
**Agent:** Bilkul! Term insurance ek bahut hi samajhdari ka faisla hai. Aapki umar kitni hai aur aap kitna cover lena chahte hain?

**Lead:** 32 saal, 1 crore cover chahiye.
**Agent:** 32 saal ki umar mein 1 crore ka term cover approximately ₹800-1200 per month mein mil sakta hai. Kya aap smoker hain ya non-smoker?

**Lead:** Non-smoker.
**Agent:** Bahut achha! Aapke liye kaafi achhe options hain. Kya main aapko hamare advisor se milwa sakta hoon jo aapko best plan suggest kar sakein?

**Lead:** Haan, please.
**Agent:** Main aapka naam aur best time to call note kar leta hoon.

## What the Agent Handles
- Term, health, motor, home insurance queries
- Premium estimation (ballpark)
- Policy comparison in simple language
- Lead detail collection (name, age, contact, requirement)
- Callback booking with advisor
- Document checklist sharing
- Common insurance myth busting

Agent provides general information only — does not give specific financial advice. All recommendations are made by your licensed advisor after callback.
