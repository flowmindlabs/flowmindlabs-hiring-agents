# Complaint Handler Agent

## What It Does
Receives customer complaints, acknowledges them with empathy, logs every complaint, resolves simple issues automatically, and escalates complex ones to your team — with full conversation context so no customer has to repeat themselves.

## Who It Is For
- E-commerce businesses
- Food delivery and restaurants
- Service companies
- Hospitality
- Any business with post-sale customer issues

## Sample Conversations

**Customer:** My order arrived damaged. This is unacceptable.
**Agent:** I am really sorry to hear that. That is absolutely not the experience we want you to have. Can you share a photo of the damaged item? I will get this resolved for you right away.

**Customer:** [Sends photo]
**Agent:** Thank you. I have logged your complaint (Ref: #CMP2046). Our team will contact you within 2 hours with a resolution — replacement or full refund. Again, I sincerely apologise for this.

## What the Agent Handles
- Complaint acknowledgment with empathy
- Complaint logging with reference number
- Photo and evidence collection
- Simple resolutions (tracking, status updates)
- Escalation to human with full context
- Follow-up after resolution
- Complaint trend reporting

## Escalation Rules
- Damaged product → Collect evidence → Escalate to ops team
- Delivery delay → Check status → Update customer
- Refund request → Verify order → Process or escalate
- Abusive language → De-escalate → Escalate to manager
