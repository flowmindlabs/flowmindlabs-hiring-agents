# WhatsApp Support Agent

## What It Does
Handles all incoming customer queries on WhatsApp, Telegram, or Web Chat — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Answers questions, shares information, and escalates to a human when needed — with full conversation context.

## Who It Is For
- Retail shops
- Restaurants and cafes
- Salons and spas
- Clinics and hospitals
- E-commerce businesses
- Any business receiving repetitive customer queries

## Sample Conversations

**Customer:** Hi, are you open on Sunday?
**Agent:** Yes! We are open Sunday from 10am to 8pm. How can I help you today?

**Customer:** What is the price of the XL size?
**Agent:** The XL size is ₹1,299. Would you like to place an order?

**Customer:** I have a complaint about my last order.
**Agent:** I am sorry to hear that. Let me connect you with our team right away. [Escalates with full context]
