# Order Tracking Agent

## What It Does
Customer sends their order ID or phone number — agent fetches the current status and replies instantly. No more calls asking "where is my order?" — handled automatically 24/7.

## Who It Is For
- E-commerce businesses
- Logistics and courier companies
- Food delivery
- Retail with home delivery
- Any business that ships orders

## Sample Conversations

**Customer:** Where is my order? Order ID 45821
**Agent:** Your order #45821 is out for delivery! Expected delivery today between 2pm and 6pm. Track live here: [link]

**Customer:** Mera order kab aayega? 9876543210

**Customer:** My order shows delivered but I haven't received it.
